Few Words About Us

Shree Paschimbhumi is a residential English medium school established in 2053 B.S. guided by an educational philosophy that promotes the development of the whole person blending academics, athletics and creative achievements. The school aims at providing opportunities and moulds its students into confident individuals, ready to take challenges in every phase of life. PEBS is committed to providing a safe and secure environment where students enjoy learning and recognise their worth & dignity. The PEBS helps students learn in the ways that are right for them, nurturing a joy for learning and a passion to pursue their interests.

Our Vision

We believe in each individual to develop into a Global citizen who have knowledge, compassion, leadership and are empowered to stand up to the challenges to understand our world and improve conditions for local and global communities.

PEBS Mission

“To nurture inquiring, knowledgeable and caring young lifelong learners who are engaged citizens of our world through intercultural understanding and respect.”

Message from Principal

Dear Students and Guardians,
Welcome to the 2020-21 school year! Shree Paschimbhumi School prides itself in being a place that supports the positive growth of each and every student. We want all students to experience success and to have a purposeful connection to our faculty and staff. Our school will provide both academic and social-emotional supports that our students require as we reach out to support the family unit through academics, sports, community involvement, and extra-curricular activities. Please encourage your son/daughter to get involved and stay connected. We want all parents and guardians to feel supported at the school and encourage parents and guardians to call the school to access resources and to inquire about the progress of your son or daughter. Paschimbhumi School has a plethora of academic and behavioral resources to help support students and families. All incoming freshmen will be enrolled in a course entitled, Choices: School & Career that will prepare students for leading a successful and fulfilling life through the thoughtful creation. By the end of their senior year, students will graduate with a complete knowledge and life plan.
Sincerely,

Services

At PEBS, we aim to give every child the very best start possible in their education through vibrant and engaging teaching. We enable the children to become active learners by offering challenging, broad and balanced International Baccalaureate and CDC programs.

Quality Education

All our facilitators ensure the best possible quality of education for the children in every year group.

Hostel

The PEBS Hostel has live-in teacher supervisors who are each assigned a group of students to support, plus school counsellors.

Laboratories

PEBS has a laboratory for each and every type of activity that requires it-notable among them at present being the Science, Computer and Multimedia Labs.
